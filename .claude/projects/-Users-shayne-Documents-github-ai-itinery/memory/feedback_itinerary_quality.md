Never generate weak itineraries. The AI must think like an experienced travel planner:
- Never put a single activity alone on a day (e.g., a river cruise can't be the only thing)
- Prioritize iconic must-see attractions first (Paris = Eiffel Tower on Day 1, obviously)
- Analyze what travelers actually expect from a destination and deliver that
- Each day must feel complete: 3-4 activities with meals, logical geographic flow
- Think about the emotional arc of the trip: start with the iconic highlights, then deeper exploration

**Why:** User found it "terrible" when AI put a Seine cruise alone on a full day and didn't prioritize the Eiffel Tower. The AI must be a "situation analyst" — understand traveler psychology.

**How to apply:** The itinerary prompt must include explicit instructions about prioritizing iconic sights, filling days properly, and thinking about what makes a trip memorable. This applies to both eco and pro mode prompts.
